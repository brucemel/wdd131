ABOUT US

Interplast is a new brand of plastic products entering the retail market. We started in 2020 as a small company and have now expanded to Chiclayo. Mainly because we have a wide variety of household products. The advantage of working with us is that we deliver anywhere and we can eventually leave the merchandise on credit without additional charges (for now) if it's overdue. We have everything you need.
